About Lab Results Viewer

This tool helps you visualise and understand your lab test results over time. Upload a CSV or XML export from your health portal (e.g. ManageMyHealth) and see trends, reference ranges, and clinical patterns at a glance.

📊 Sex-Specific Reference Ranges

Many lab tests have different normal ranges depending on biological sex. Reference ranges from your doctor or lab should already be tailored to your age and sex — in most cases they will be accurate as-is.

However, some exports provide generic ranges or omit them entirely. The Male / Female toggle lets you apply population-specific reference ranges to the following 14 tests:

⚗️ Axis Analysis

The Axis Analysis feature is an experimental educational tool that looks for patterns across multiple test results. It evaluates 50 clinical patterns by comparing your latest values against reference ranges — for example, detecting possible iron deficiency when both ferritin and haemoglobin are low.

Each pattern is categorised by severity:

  • Info — Observations worth noting but not necessarily concerning
  • Warning — Patterns that may warrant further discussion with your doctor
  • Alert — Significant patterns that should be reviewed by a healthcare professional

Patterns that require tests you haven't uploaded are shown as greyed out with the missing tests listed.
